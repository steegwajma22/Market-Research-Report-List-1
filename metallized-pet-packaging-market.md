<p><p>Metallized PET packaging is an advanced form of plastic packaging that combines the properties of polyethylene terephthalate (PET) with a thin layer of metal, typically aluminum. This type of packaging is widely used in the food and beverage industry due to its excellent barrier properties, which protect products from moisture, light, and oxygen, thus extending shelf life. Metallized PET is lightweight, cost-effective, and fully recyclable, making it an appealing choice for environmentally conscious consumers and companies.</p><p>The Metallized PET Packaging Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
