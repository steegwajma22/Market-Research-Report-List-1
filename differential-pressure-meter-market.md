<p><strong>The Differential Pressure Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston Style</li><li>Diaphragm Type</li><li>Bellows differential Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The differential pressure meter market encompasses various types, including piston style, diaphragm type, and bellows differential types. Piston style meters utilize a moving piston to measure pressure differences and are known for their accuracy in higher pressure ranges. Diaphragm type meters employ a flexible diaphragm that reacts to pressure changes, providing reliable measurements for low to moderate pressure applications. Bellows differential meters utilize an expandable bellows structure, ideal for measuring small pressure differentials, often in harsh environments. Each type caters to different industrial needs based on specific pressure and environmental conditions.</p></p>
