<p><p>Knife organizers can be categorized into two main market types: vertical and horizontal. Horizontal organizers, on the other hand, are designed to lay flat, offering ample storage for a larger set of knives and often including compartments for other kitchen tools. Each type caters to different kitchen layouts and user preferences, optimizing organization and accessibility.</p></p>
