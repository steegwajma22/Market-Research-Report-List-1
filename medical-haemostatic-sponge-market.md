<p><p>A Medical Haemostatic Sponge is a specialized surgical product used to promote hemostasis, or the cessation of bleeding, during medical procedures. Their use is pivotal in various surgical fields, including orthopedics, cardiovascular, and trauma care.</p><p>The Medical Haemostatic Sponge Market is anticipated to witness significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period.
