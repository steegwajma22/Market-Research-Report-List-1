<p><p>The 5G mmWave chipset market is categorized into two main types: Baseband Processor and Radio Frequency Integrated Circuit (RFIC). The Baseband Processor handles signal processing and communication protocols, enabling efficient data transmission and reception. In contrast, RFIC manages the conversion of digital signals to analog and vice versa, facilitating high-frequency operations essential for mmWave technology. Together, these components ensure the robust performance required for ultra-high-speed 5G networks, enhancing capacity and reducing latency for various applications.</p></p>
