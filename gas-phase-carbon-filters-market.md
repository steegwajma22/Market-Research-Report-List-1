<p><p>Gas phase carbon filters are essential for air purification and odor removal in various industries. Granular carbon filters utilize loose granules of activated carbon, allowing for high surface area interaction with contaminants. Bonded carbon filters feature carbon particles adhered together, enhancing structural integrity and improving flow characteristics. Activated carbon filters, known for their high adsorption capacity, effectively trap pollutants and volatile organic compounds. Each type serves specific applications based on efficiency, design, and intended use in gas treatment systems.</p></p>
