<p><strong>The Cardiac Monitoring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ECG Method</li><li>Arterial Pressure Method</li><li>Photoelectric Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac monitoring devices market consists of three primary types: </p><p>1. **ECG Method**: This method records the electrical activity of the heart, providing insights into heart rhythm and diagnosing cardiac conditions.</p><p>2. **Arterial Pressure Method**: This involves measuring blood pressure within arteries, offering critical data on cardiovascular health and circulatory efficiency.</p><p>3. **Photoelectric Method**: Utilizing light absorption changes in blood, this non-invasive technique assesses heart rate and oxygen saturation, aiding in continuous monitoring and diagnostics. Together, these approaches enhance patient care and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
