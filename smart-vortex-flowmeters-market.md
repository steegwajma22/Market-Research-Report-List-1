<p><p>Smart vortex flowmeters are advanced devices used for measuring fluid flow, categorized into three main types. Inline vortex flowmeters are installed directly in the pipeline, providing accurate readings for continuous flow. Mass flow vortex flowmeters measure the mass of the fluid, ensuring precise data for applications requiring accurate mass calculations. Insertion vortex flowmeters allow for easy installation and maintenance by being inserted into existing pipelines, making them versatile solutions for various industrial processes, enhancing efficiency and reducing operational costs.</p></p>
