<p><p>The Automotive Grade 3D LiDAR Sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid-State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR employs rotating components to capture detailed 3D maps, offering high resolution but potentially more complexities and size constraints. In contrast, Solid-State LiDAR utilizes fixed components without moving parts, making it more compact, durable, and cost-effective for mass production. These technologies are essential for applications like autonomous driving, enhancing safety and navigation capabilities in vehicles.</p></p>
