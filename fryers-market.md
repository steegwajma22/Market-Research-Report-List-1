<p><p>Fryers are categorized into two main types based on their installation: countertop and floor-mounted. Countertop fryers are compact and designed for limited kitchen space, making them suitable for small establishments or for additional frying capacity in larger kitchens. Floor-mounted fryers, on the other hand, are larger and more robust, ideal for high-volume frying in commercial kitchens. They typically offer greater oil capacity and can handle more significant cooking demands, making them a preferred choice for restaurants and catering services.</p></p>
