<p><p>Asphalt distributors are essential in road construction, primarily categorized into drag and self-propelled types. Drag asphalt distributors are towed behind a vehicle, offering a cost-effective solution for smaller projects with moderate application needs. In contrast, self-propelled asphalt distributors are equipped with their own propulsion system, allowing for greater maneuverability and precision in applying asphalt materials. These machines cater to larger projects, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor intensity while ensuring a uniform distribution of asphalt across various surfaces.</p></p>
