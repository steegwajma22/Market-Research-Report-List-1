<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Toasted Malted Wheat Flour Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diastatic Toasted Malted Wheat Flour</li><li>Non-Diastatic Toasted Malted Wheat Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The toasted malted wheat flour market is segmented into diastatic and non-diastatic types. Diastatic toasted malted wheat flour contains active enzymes that can break down starches into sugars, enhancing fermentation and flavor, making it ideal for baking and brewing. Non-diastatic toasted malted wheat flour, on the other hand, lacks these enzymes and is primarily used for its rich flavor and color in various food products, without influencing fermentation processes. Both types cater to distinct applications in the food industry.</p></p>
