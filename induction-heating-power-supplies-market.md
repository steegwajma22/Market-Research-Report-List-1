<p><p>Induction heating power supplies are categorized into three main types: IF (Intermediate Frequency) induction power supplies, which operate typically between 1 kHz to 100 kHz and are suitable for applications like metal hardening; High Frequency induction power supplies, ranging from 100 kHz to 1 MHz, ideal for processes such as melting and brazing; and Ultra High Frequency induction power supplies, exceeding 1 MHz, often used in specialized applications like micro-processing and food heating. Each type is tailored to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
