<p><p>The Touch Paint market can be categorized into two main types: water-based and solvent-based Touch Paint. Water-based Touch Paint utilizes water as the primary solvent, offering easy cleanup and lower levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making it more environmentally friendly. In contrast, solvent-based Touch Paint relies on organic solvents, providing quicker drying times and durability, often preferred for heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and project requirements, balancing performance, safety, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
