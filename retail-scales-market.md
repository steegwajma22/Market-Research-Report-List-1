<p><p>Retail scales are categorized into dual-display and single-display types. Dual-display scales feature two screens, allowing both the customer and the cashier to view the weight and price simultaneously, enhancing transparency and customer interaction. In contrast, single-display scales have one screen, typically facing the cashier, which may limit customer awareness of weight and pricing. Both types cater to different retail environments and customer needs, with dual-display models often preferred in high-traffic settings for improved communication and service efficiency.</p></p>
