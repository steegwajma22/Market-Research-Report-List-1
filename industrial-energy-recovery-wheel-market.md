<p><strong>The Industrial Energy Recovery Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensation Rotors</li><li>Hygroscopic Rotors</li><li>Sorption Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Energy Recovery Wheel market comprises three main types: Condensation Rotors, Hygroscopic Rotors, and Sorption Rotors. Condensation Rotors focus on recovering energy through the condensation of moisture from exhaust air, enhancing HVAC efficiency. Hygroscopic Rotors utilize materials that absorb moisture, transferring it to drier air streams for improved energy conservation. Sorption Rotors combine adsorption and desorption processes to recover latent heat from exhaust air. Together, these technologies contribute to energy efficiency in industrial applications, reducing operational costs and environmental impact.</p></p>
