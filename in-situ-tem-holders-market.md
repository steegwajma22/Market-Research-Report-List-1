<p><p>In situ TEM holders are specialized tools used in transmission electron microscopy for studying materials under specific conditions. Single tilt in situ TEM holders allow for one-axis tilting, providing limited specimen orientation and analysis. Both types are crucial for investigating dynamic processes in materials, such as phase transitions and structural changes, enhancing the understanding of nanoscale phenomena.</p></p>
