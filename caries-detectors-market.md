<p><p>The caries detectors market comprises methods like transillumination and laser fluorescence for early dental caries detection. Transillumination utilizes light to reveal carious lesions by identifying changes in tooth translucency, allowing for minimally invasive diagnosis. Laser fluorescence employs specific wavelengths of light to excite dental tissues, with carious areas fluorescing differently than healthy ones, aiding in precise detection. Both technologies enhance diagnostic accuracy, facilitate timely treatment, and contribute to improved patient outcomes by enabling early intervention in dental care.</p></p>
