<p><p>Polymer microinjection molding involves the production of small, precise plastic components using various polymer types. Polyoxymethylene (POM) offers excellent dimensional stability, while Liquid Crystal Polymer (LCP) provides advanced mechanical properties. Polylactic Acid (PLA), a biodegradable option, is popular in sustainable applications. Other polymers also cater to specialized needs within the market.</p></p>
