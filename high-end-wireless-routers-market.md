<p><p>The high-end wireless routers market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Single Band Wireless Routers** operate on one frequency (typically 2.4 GHz), offering basic connectivity suitable for low-demand applications.</p><p>2. **Dual Band Wireless Routers** support both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz bands, providing improved performance and reduced interference for multiple devices and higher bandwidth tasks.</p><p>3. **Tri Band Wireless Routers** include an additional 5 GHz band, optimizing bandwidth distribution for more devices, enhancing overall speed and network performance in busy environments.</p></p>
