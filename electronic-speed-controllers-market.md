<p><p>The Electronic Speed Controller (ESC) market is primarily divided into two types: brushed and brushless ESCs. Brushed ESCs are designed for traditional brushed motors, providing simple control for applications like entry-level RC vehicles. They are generally more affordable but less efficient. In contrast, brushless ESCs are used with advanced brushless motors, offering higher efficiency, longer lifespan, and more precise control, making them ideal for professional-grade models and drones. The choice between them depends on performance requirements and application suitability.</p></p>
