<p><strong>The Ball Screws for New Energy Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating Ball Screw</li><li>Non-circulating Ball Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball screws are essential components in new energy vehicles, enabling precise linear motion and efficiency. The circulating ball screw features recirculating balls within a closed circuit, allowing continuous motion and smoother operation, ideal for applications requiring high durability and efficiency. In contrast, non-circulating ball screws utilize balls that do not return to the nut, often suitable for shorter travel applications where simplicity and cost-effectiveness are prioritized. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing performance and drive mechanisms in electric and hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
