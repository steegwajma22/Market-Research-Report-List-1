<p><strong>The Powder Coating Gun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Corona Powder Coating Gun</li><li>Tribo Powder Coating Gun</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powder coating gun market primarily consists of two types: Corona powder coating guns and Tribo powder coating guns. Corona guns utilize an electrostatic corona effect to apply charged powder particles onto the workpiece, offering a uniform coating and efficient transfer efficiency. In contrast, Tribo guns rely on friction to charge the powder particles, which can enhance coverage in complex geometries and is effective for thicker coatings. Both types are essential in various industries for delivering durable, high-quality finishes.</p></p>
