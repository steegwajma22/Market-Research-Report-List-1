<p><p>The guitar looper market is categorized into two primary types: floor-based and desktop models. Floor-based loopers are designed for live performances and feature durable footswitch controls, allowing musicians to create and manipulate loops seamlessly while playing. These devices often emphasize portability and easy access. In contrast, desktop type loopers are aimed at studio use, providing advanced editing features and connectivity options for integration with other equipment. They typically offer greater functionality but are less suited for live performance settings.</p></p>
