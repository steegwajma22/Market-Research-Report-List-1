<p><p>The Variable Refrigerant Volume (VRV) system market is primarily categorized into two types: Heat Recovery Systems and Heat Pump Systems. Heat Recovery Systems allow simultaneous heating and cooling in different zones, optimizing energy use by transferring excess heat. In contrast, Heat Pump Systems focus on either heating or cooling mode, making them more efficient for single-zone applications. Both systems are favored for their energy efficiency and flexibility, catering to diverse commercial and residential needs in HVAC solutions.</p></p>
