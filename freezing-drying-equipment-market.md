Additionally, the rising trend of personalized medicine and the demand for high-quality biologics are further propelling market growth. </p><p>As industries increasingly focus on sustainability and reducing waste, freeze-drying plays a pivotal role by preserving the nutritional value of food and extending the shelf life of sensitive products. <p><p>The freeze drying equipment market encompasses three primary types: industrial-scale freeze dryers, pilot-scale freeze dryers, and lab-scale freeze dryers. Industrial-scale freeze dryers are designed for large production volumes and are used in commercial applications, while pilot-scale freeze dryers serve as intermediary systems for process development and small batch production. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
