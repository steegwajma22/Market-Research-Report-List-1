<p><p>The dental CBCT market is segmented into three types based on field of view (FOV): Large FOV, Medium FOV, and Small FOV. Large FOV systems capture comprehensive scans of entire jaws and surrounding anatomy, making them ideal for complex cases like implant planning and orthodontics. Medium FOV targets specific areas, balancing detail and coverage for procedures such as endodontics. Small FOV systems provide high-resolution images of localized areas, perfect for precise evaluations in single-tooth analysis and root canal treatments.</p></p>
