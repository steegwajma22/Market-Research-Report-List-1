<p><p>The Education Data Security market is categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based security utilizes remote servers to store and manage educational data, offering scalability, ease of access, and often lower upfront costs. It enables institutions to implement updates and security measures rapidly. Conversely, on-premise security involves hosting data on local servers within the institution's facilities, providing greater control and customization. However, it typically requires higher initial investment and more extensive IT management.</p></p>
