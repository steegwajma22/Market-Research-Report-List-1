<p><strong>The Dewatering Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible Dewatering Pumps</li><li>Non-Submersible Dewatering Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dewatering pump market is categorized into submersible and non-submersible types. Submersible dewatering pumps are designed to operate submerged in water, effectively removing liquids from deep excavations, basements, or flood sites. They offer high efficiency and easy handling. Non-submersible dewatering pumps, on the other hand, are placed above the water level and use suction to draw out water, suitable for various industrial applications. Both types are essential for managing water levels in construction, mining, and municipal sectors.</p></p>
