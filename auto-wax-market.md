<p><p>The auto wax market consists of two primary types: synthetic and natural waxes. Synthetic waxes are formulated using polymers and chemical compounds, offering enhanced durability, smooth application, and resistance to environmental factors. These provide a long-lasting shine and protection for vehicles. On the other hand, natural waxes, derived from natural sources like carnauba, provide a traditional finish with a rich depth of shine.
