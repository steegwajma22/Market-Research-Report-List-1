They are effective in high-temperature environments. Water-soluble passivators, on the other hand, are utilized in aqueous applications, such as cooling systems and metalworking fluids, where they inhibit corrosion and prolong equipment lifespan. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing performance and durability across various industrial applications.</p></p>
