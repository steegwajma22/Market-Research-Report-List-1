<p><p>The luminescence detectors market comprises various types, with tube luminescence detectors and microplate luminescence detectors being prominent. Tube luminescence detectors utilize glass or plastic tubes for sample analysis, offering high sensitivity and specificity for detecting light emissions in small volumes. In contrast, microplate luminescence detectors are designed for high-throughput applications, enabling simultaneous analysis of multiple samples in microplate formats. Both types play vital roles in biomedical research, drug discovery, and environmental monitoring, facilitating efficient luminescence measurements across diverse applications.</p></p>
