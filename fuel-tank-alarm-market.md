<p><strong>The Fuel Tank Alarm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Type</li><li>Float Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel tank alarm market includes various technologies, primarily the ultrasonic and float types. Ultrasonic alarms use sound waves to detect fuel levels, providing precise measurements and non-contact capability, ideal for challenging environments. In contrast, float-type alarms rely on a buoyant device that rises and falls with fuel levels, triggering alerts when thresholds are reached. Both types ensure tank monitoring, prevent overflows, and enhance safety, catering to industries like transportation, construction, and fuel storage.</p></p>
