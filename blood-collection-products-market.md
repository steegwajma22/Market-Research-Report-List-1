<p><strong>The Blood Collection Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Serum Separating Tubes</li><li>EDTA Tubes</li><li>Plasma Separation Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood collection products market encompasses various tube types instrumental for laboratory analysis. Serum separating tubes facilitate the coagulation of blood, allowing serum extraction for tests. EDTA tubes, containing ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid, are designed for collecting whole blood samples, preventing clotting, and are essential for hematology and blood bank procedures. Plasma separation tubes contain gel or additives that separate plasma after centrifugation, ensuring high-quality plasma samples for various tests. Each type serves a specific purpose in diagnostics and research.</p></p>
