<p><p>The tea filter bag paper market is categorized into heat-sealable and non-heat-sealable types. Heat-sealable filter bags utilize thermoplastic materials that bond when heat is applied, offering a secure seal and preventing leakage during steeping. These are commonly used in commercial tea production for convenience and consistency. In contrast, non-heat-sealable bags rely on adhesive or mechanical methods for sealing, providing more flexibility in design and usage. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and production methods, enhancing the tea packaging experience.</p></p>
