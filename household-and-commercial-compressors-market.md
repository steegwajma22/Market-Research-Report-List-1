<p><p>The household and commercial compressors market is segmented into various types based on design and application. Reciprocating compressors use pistons for compression and are common in refrigeration. Rotary compressors are compact and efficient, ideal for air conditioning. Screw compressors use two helical screws for continuous airflow, making them suitable for industrial applications. Scroll compressors employ spiral designs for quiet operation and energy efficiency. Centrifugal compressors leverage high-speed rotation to compress large volumes of gas, ideal for larger industrial applications.</p></p>
