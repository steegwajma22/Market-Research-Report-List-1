<p><p>The multi-mode microplate reader market includes three primary types: absorbance, fluorescence, and luminescence. Absorbance readers measure the amount of light absorbed by a sample, useful for quantifying concentrations. Fluorescence readers detect emitted light from excited molecules, allowing for sensitive detection of specific targets. Luminescence readers measure light produced by chemical reactions, often used in assays and drug discovery. These technologies are essential for various applications in research, clinical diagnostics, and pharmaceutical development, enhancing the precision and versatility of experimental analysis.</p></p>
