<p><p>Location marketing encompasses strategies that utilize geographic data to engage consumers based on their location. Indoor geo-marketing focuses on targeting customers within buildings, utilizing technologies like Bluetooth beacons and Wi-Fi to deliver personalized promotions, enhance navigation, and improve in-store experiences. Outdoor geo-marketing, on the other hand, targets potential customers in open spaces, leveraging GPS and mobile data for location-based advertising, driving foot traffic to businesses, and optimizing delivery services. Both types aim to connect brands with consumers effectively.</p></p>
